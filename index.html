About me

I am a biomedical researcher studying pulmonary immune responses and host–pathogen interactions during bacterial respiratory infections.

My research focuses particularly on cystic fibrosis, non-tuberculous mycobacteria, Mycobacterium abscessus and other emerging bacterial pathogens.

Research

Our work combines experimental infection models, human respiratory samples and advanced omics technologies to investigate the spatial organization of immune and bacterial responses within infected tissues.

Research interests

  • Host–pathogen interactions
  • Respiratory diseases
  • Respiratory infections
  • Mycobacteria
  • Gram-negative
  • In vitro and in vivo models
  • Dual host–pathogen transcriptomics
