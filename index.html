About me

I am a biomedical researcher studying pulmonary immune responses and host–pathogen interactions. Currently, I am a Project Leader working on my independent research program within the Emerging Bacterial Pathogens Unit at DITID-IRCCS Ospedale San Raffaele (OSR) (Milan, Italy).

Our work combines experimental infection models, human respiratory samples and advanced omics technologies to investigate the spatial organization of immune and bacterial responses within infected tissues

Research

My research focuses particularly on chronic respiratory diseses and lung infections, with a particular interests on opportunistc pathogens. My scientific interests focus on the development of novel therapeutic approaches/interventions to modulate host/pathogen response in experimental models (E.g. in vitro, exvivo and in vivo), with a particular attention on chronic respiratory infections and opportunistic pathogens (nontuberculous mycobacteria, NTHi or P. aeruginosa). My research activity has been, or it is supported by national and international grant agency, such Italian Ministry of health, Fondazione Cariplo, Fondazione Italiana Fibrosi cistica, Telethon or US cystic fibrosis foundation. .
