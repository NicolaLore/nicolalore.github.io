About me

I am a biomedical researcher studying pulmonary immune responses and host–pathogen interactions.

My research focuses particularly on chronic respiratory diseses and lung infections, with a particular interests on opportunistc pathogens

Research

Our work combines experimental infection models, human respiratory samples and advanced omics technologies to investigate the spatial organization of immune and bacterial responses within infected tissues.

Research interests

  • Host–pathogen interactions
  • Respiratory diseases
  • Respiratory infections
  • Mycobacteria
  • Gram-negative bacteria
  • In vitro and in vivo models
  • Dual host–pathogen transcriptomics
  • Antimicrobial and immune therapies
